|upakramagāthāḥ |śrutvaitaduttamaṃ sthānaṃ bhūmyāḥ śreṣṭhaṃ manoramam |prasannamanasaṃkalpaharṣitāḥ
sugatātmajāḥ

abhyutthitā āsanebhya abhyudgamya khagapathe |abhyokiranti kusumaiḥ sādhviti vyāharī girā

sādhu sādhu mahāprājña vajragarbha viśārada |yannirdiṣṭā tvayā bhūmi bodhisattvāna yā carī

parṣaddhi viprasannā tu vimukticandraḥ pṛcchati |uttariṃ kīrtiyā bhūmiṃ dvitīyāṃ sugatātmajāḥ

kīdṛśā manasaṃkalpā dvitīyāmabhilakṣataḥ |pravyāhara mahāprājña śrotukāmā jinātmajāḥ

upasaṃhāragāthāḥ |te mārdavārjavamṛdūkarmaṇīyacittāḥkalyāṇaāśaya damāśayatābhyupetāḥ |saṃsargapekṣavigatāśca
udārabuddhimāhātmya āśayavid dvitīyākramanti

atra sthitā guṇadharāḥ kuśalopapetāḥprāṇātipātavigatā avihiṃsacittāḥ |adattadānapagatāḥ paradāratāṃ
casatyānvitā apiśunaḥ puruṣapradhānāḥ

parabhogabhidyavigatā vidu maitracittāḥsamyakpathe upagatā aśaṭhajñakāśca |nirmāṇakāyagrahaṇāśca
supeśalāścarakṣanti śāstuśaraṇaṃ sada apramattāḥ

duḥkhāni yāni niraye tatha tiryagyonauyamaśāsane jvalitaāśrayanityupetāḥ |sarve ti pāpapatitākṣalāḥ
prabhontihantā vivarjiya upemahi satyadharmam

ādau ca kṛtva manujānupapattimiṣṭāṃyāvadbhavāgramaraṇāśayadhyānu śikṣām |pratyekayānamatha śrāvakabuddhayānaṃsarve
ito daśabhi śuklapathaiḥ prabhūtam

evaṃ viditva satataṃ vidu apramattāḥśīleṣu saṃsthita parānapi sthāpayanti |bhūyottare karuṇaāśayatābhyupetāḥsattvān
viditva dukhitān kṛpa saṃjanenti

hanto vidṛṣṭipatitā imi bālabuddhīkrodhopanāhadrutacitta vivādaprāptāḥ |satataṃ atṛpta viṣaye
bhuyu prārthayantitrinidāna sattva parimocayitavya ete

mahaandhakāratamasāvṛta mohachannāḥkāntāramārgapatitā mahadṛṣṭijāle |saṃsārapañjaragatā ripu
dharṣayantimokṣāmyahaṃ namucipañjaramadhyaprāptān

kleśormibhihriyata oghacaturnimagnātraidhātuke dukhaśataiḥ paripīḍyamānāḥ |skandhālayābhyupagatā
vṛtaātmasaṃjñāteṣārthi yujyami ahaṃ dukhamocanārtham

avasṛjya śreṣṭhapravaraṃ ima buddhajñānaṃsati eva niḥsaraṇi hīnamatiṃ janenti |sthāpemi tān
vimalajñāni tathāgatānāṃvīryārabhanti atulaṃ vidu bodhihetoḥ

atra sthitā guṇaśatopacitā maharṣipaśyanti naikasugatānapi pūjayanti |teṣāṃ śubhaṃ bhuyu uttapyati
kalpakoṭyāṃkāsīsakāñcanavaraṃ ca yathā nikṣiptam

atra sthitā jinasutā nṛpacakravartibhūtvā praṇenti daśabhiḥ kuśalebhi sattvān |yaccaiva saṃci
śubhasaṃcaya saṃcinantitrātā bhavema jagato daśabhirbalāḍhyaiḥ

ākāṅkṣamāṇa vijahitva ca rajabhogānpravrajya śāsanavare upagamya dhīrāḥ |vīryānvitā labhiya
śreṣṭhavaraṃ samādhiṃbuddhā sahasra paripūrṇa kṣeṇe dṛśanti

evaṃvidhā gaṇanayā bhuyu anya nekāādarśayanti vṛṣabhī sthita atra bhūmau |ata uttari praṇidhijñānavarābhyupetānaikā
vikurvitavidhau vinayanti sattvān

ityeṣā dvitiyā bhūmirnirdiṣṭā sugatātmajāḥ |sarvalokahitaiṣīṇāṃ bodhisattvānanuttamā
