|upakramagāthāḥ |atha vividharucirameghān marudgaṇo'bhikiriṣu vegaprāptāḥ |pravyāharanti madhurā
girivara śubha prītisaṃpūrṇāḥ

sādhu varatīkṣṇacittā guṇaśatasamupetajñānavaśavartim |varacaraṇaṃ parituṣṭaṃ jagahitavarapuṇḍarīkāṇām

tada pravaramatulamābhā maheśvarāḥ khegatā naravarasya |vararuciragandhameghānabhikiri kleśaughamapahartum

pravyāharanti madhuraṃ marudgaṇā harṣakararuciraghoṣāḥ |paramasulabdhalābhāḥ śrutu yairayu bhūminirdeśaḥ

tūrya madhuraghoṣayukta marukanyāḥ prīṇitamanobhiḥ |sucarasugatānubhāvādvaracaririyamīdṛśī proktā

sumanī sucaraṇaśreṣṭhaḥ sudānta damakāna lokamahitānām |atikramya sarvalokaṃ lokacariṃ darśayī
sūkṣmām

darśenti kāya vividhān kāyākāyāṃśca dharmatopetāḥ |śamathaḥ samitivibhakto bhaṇati ghoṣaṃ na
cākṣaraṃ ravati

kṣetraśatamākramante pūjenti nāyakān paramapūjiyān |ātmajanitakṣetrasaṃjñā vidhunitvā jñānavaśavartī

paripācayanti sattvānna cātmaparasaṃjña sarvaśa upenti |śubha saṃcinanti pravaraṃ na cāpi śubhasaṃcayaniketāḥ

rāgarajadoṣamohaiḥ paśyitva sarvaloka jvalamānān |varjeti sarvasaṃjñā vīryaṃ varamārabhī kṛpayā

marukanyā devasaṃghāśca pūjentā varasvaram |tūṣṇīṃbhāvaratāḥ sarve prekṣante puruṣarṣabham

pariṣadviprasanneyamavocat sugatātmajam |saptamyā bhūmerākārān nirdiśasva guṇākara

upasaṃhāragāthāḥ |gambhīrajñāna paramārthapadānusārīṣaḍbhūminiścitamatiḥ susamāhitātmā |prajñāmupāya
yugapadyabhinirharantobhūmyākramanti vidu saptami caryaśreṣṭhām

śūnyānimittapraṇidhīkṛpamaitrayuktābuddhānudharma sugatānuga pūjayantaḥ |jñānena śubhamahapuṇyabalebhyatṛptā-stāmākramanti
vidu saptami bhūmideśam

traidhātukena adhivāsa vivekaprāptāḥśāntaśca kleśabalaśāntijagābhikāṅkṣī |pratibhāsa māya supinādvayadharmacārīkṛpa
darśayanti vidu saptamimākramanti

śodhenti kṣetra khasamāśaya nirvikalpājinalakṣanairupāgato'caladharmatāyām |abhilāpyaghoṣavigatā
jagatoṣaṇārthaṃkṣaṇajñāna cittasya jināna samosaranti

abhāsaprāpta iti dharma vicārayantiākrānta bhūmipravarāṃ jagadarthakārāḥ |te atra bhūmyasthita
sattvacarī anantānvicinanti karma sugatān niyutāpramāṇān

kṣetrāṃśca naikavidhadharmatha kalpasaṃkhyānadhimuktiāśaya ca cittavicitradhārān |triyāṇadeśanamananta
samosarantiasmābhi sattva paripācayitavyametat

ye te jñānanicitā varamārgaprāptāīryāpathaiścaturbhi prajñamupāyamuktāḥ |sarvasmi cittakṣaṇi
bodhiguṇānuprāptāḥparipūrayanti daśa pāramitāpradeśān

sarveṣu mārgakuśalasya ya eṣa dānaṃśīlaṃ ca kleśapraśamaṃ kṣamamakṣatitvam |vīryaṃ ca bhūyu
anu uttari ārabhantemārge acalyataya dhyānaguṇānvitānām

anutpādakṣānti virajā varaprajña śreṣṭhāparṇāmupāya praṇidhī bhuyu kāṅkṣi lakṣmī |ato'mardayitva
balajñānanitīraṇatvādevaṃ khu bodhiguṇa sarvakṣaṇenupenti

ālambanātu prathamā guṇapāripūridvitīyā malāpanaya ūrdhva vibandhacchedam |caturthāya mārgu
samatākriya pañcamāyaanutpāda āhvaya viduḥ puna ṣaṣṭhavṛttiḥ

iha saptamīmupagatāḥ sakalaṃ guṇānipraṇidhāna naikavividhānabhinirharanti |kiṃ kāraṇaṃ yaduta
jñānakriyābhyupentisā aṣṭamīprabhṛti sarvaviśuddhyupenti

duratikramā dūraṃgamā bahusthānakarmākṣetrāntaradvipathameva yathottaranti |vicaranti saptasu
alipta nṛpo yathaivamārgasthitā na puna sarvatikrānta dhīrāḥ

yada aṣṭamīmupagatāḥ puna jñānabhūmimatikrānta cittaviṣaye sthita jñānakarme |brahmā na pekṣati
jagannaramānuṣātmāevaṃ caranti vidu padmamivā aliptāḥ

atra sthitā vividhakleśamatikramantiteṣāṃ na kleśacari no ca kṣayo'nuprāptiḥ |mārgasthitā na
tada kleśacariṃ carantisaṃpūrṇa āśaya jinajña kṣayo na tāvat

ye laukikā vividhaśilpakriyāprayogāājāti sarvavidunā sthita śāstrajñāne |dhyānā abhijña bala
bhāvayanto'bhyupentibhūyaḥ samādhi vividhānabhinirharanti

atikrānta śravakacariṃ tatha pratyayānāṃsthita bodhisattvacaraṇe vidu apramāṇām |pūrve hi āśayatayā
iha jñānatāyānṛpatīsuto yatha vivṛddhabalopapetaḥ

gāmbhīryatāmupagatā bhuyu ārabhanticittaṃ nirodhupagatā na ca sākṣikriyāḥ |yathā sāgare upagatāḥ
sthita yānapātrepratyakṣa sarva udake na ca yānahāniḥ

bhūyo upāyabalaprajñavarābhyupetādurjñeyasarvajagajñānakriyāguṇāḍhyāḥ |pūjenti buddha niyutā
bhuyu śuddhibhāvāyathā tadvibhūṣaṇavicitritu naikaratnaiḥ

atra sthitāna vidunāṃ varaprajña ābhāśoṣenti tṛṣṇasalilaṃ yatha bhāskārābhāḥ |te atra bhūmyupagatā
vaśavartinaścabhonti kṛtī kuśala jñānaphalodeśaiḥ

ākāṅkṣamāṇa dṛḍhavīryabalābhyupetāḥkoṭīnayūtaśata buddhasahasra pūrṇān |paśyanti sarvadiśatāsu
samāhitatvādbhūyo'pyataḥ praṇidhiśreṣṭha guṇāprameyāḥ

durjñeyā sarvalokena vaśipratyekacāribhiḥ |ityeṣā saptamī bhūmirupāyaprajñaśodhanā
