Daśabhūmikasūtre gāthāvibhāgaḥ |1 pramuditā nāma prathamā bhūmiḥ |upakramaḥ |te śukladharmupacitāḥ
kuśalopapetāḥparyupāsitāḥ sugatamaitrakṛpānukūlāḥ |adhimuktyudāra kuśalāśaya śuddhabhāvā-ścittaṃ
janenti atulaṃ jinajñānahetoḥ

sarvajñabuddhabalaśodhanavīryasthāmājinadharmaniṣpattijagatparitrāyaṇārthāḥ |mahākṛpocayavartanadharmacakraṃjinakṣetraśodhamupapadyati
cittaśreṣṭham

tryadhvaikavīkṣaṇavibuddhananirvikalpānānāvidhe jagati kālaviśodhanārtham |saṃkṣepasarvaguṇa
eṣitu nāyakānāmākāśatulya samudeti udāracittam

prajñādhipatya kṛpapūrvamupāyayuktamadhimukti - āśaya - viśuddha - balāpramāṇam |āsaṅgatābhimukhatā
- aparapraṇeyaṃsamatopapeta - sugataṃ varacittajātam

sahajāticittaratanaṃ sugatātmajānāmatikrānta bālacari buddhacari hyupetaḥ |jātaḥ kule daśabalāna
anodyapadyaḥsamatāṃ jine anugato niyatāgrabodhiḥ

ekasmi citta upapadyati bhūmilābhobhavate acalyu girirājasamāśayaśca |prāmodyaprītibahulaśca
prasādavāṃścautsāhavegavipulaḥ sadudagracittaḥ

saṃrambhahiṃsavigataśca akrodhanaścahrīgauravārjavataraśca susaṃvṛtaśca |jagatāyanaṃ smarati
apratimānajñānaṃprītiṃ janetyupagataspṛhameta sthānam

pañcā bhayā apagatāḥ sahabhūmilābhoājīvikā maraṇa kīrtyatha durgatiśca |parṣadbhayaṃ ca vigataṃ
tatha chambhitatvaṃkiṃ kāraṇaṃ tatha hi ātmaniketu nāsti

te chambhitatvavigatāḥ kṛpamaitrayuktāḥśraddhāsagauravahriyopagatā guṇāḍhyāḥ |rātriṃdivaṃ kuśalapakṣa
niṣevamāṇāḥsatyārtha dharmaniratā na tu kāmabhogaiḥ

śrutadharmacintakuśalā aniketacittālābhādaśīcittagatā uta bodhicittāḥ |jñānābhilāṣi balaśodhanabuddhadharmāeṣanti
pāramita varjitamāyaśāṭhyāḥ

yathāvādinastathakriyāḥ sthitasatyavākyāna tu dūṣaṇā jinakule cari bodhiśikṣām |lokakriyāya
vigatā niratā jagārthaṃśuklairatṛpta bhumayottarimārabhante

te eva dharmaniratā guṇārthayuktāabhinirharanti praṇidhiṃ jinadarśanāya |saddharmadhāraṇa upasaṃkramaṇā
ṛṣiṇāmabhinirharanti praṇīdhiṃ varacārikāyām

paripākasattvapariśodhanabuddhakṣetraṃte cāsya kṣetra sphuṭikā jinaaurasehi |ekāśayā jinasutehi
amoghatāyāḥsarvatra bālapathi buddhiya hetumarthe

etāṃśca naikapraṇidhīnabhinirharantite co anantavipulāya anantatāyai |ākāśadhātusattvadharmatanirvṛtaṃ
caloko hyaniṣṭha jinamutpadi jñānabhūmī

cittasya no viṣayajñānapraveśaniṣṭhāyā vartani trividhaniṣṭha jagatyanantā |praṇidhānaniṣṭhitu
bhavenna mamaivarūpāyatha eta niṣṭha tatha carya samā labheyam

evaṃ sunirhṛtasumārdavasnigdhacittāḥśraddheta buddhaguṇa sattva vilokayantaḥ |prītyāntulambhupagataḥ
kṛpamaitratāṃ caparitāyitavya maya sattva dukhārditāni

teṣārthi tyāga vividhaṃ puna ārabhanterājyaṃ varaṃ vividharatnahayān gajāṃśca |śirahastapādanayanā
svakamātmamāṃsaṃsarvaṃ tyajanti na ca dīnamanā bhavanti

eṣanti śāstra vividhānna ca khedamenti |śāstrajña lokacaritānyanuvartayanti |lokajñatāmupagatā
hriyatā dhṛtiṃ capūjyanti cāpratisamān gurugauraveṇa

eṣābhiyuktavidunā divarātri nityamuttapyate kuśala svarṇa yathaiva agnau |so cāpi eva parikarma
daśāna bhūmīkṛtvā asaṅgatamupeti aviṣṭhihantā

yatha sārthavāha mahasārthahitāya yuktopucchitva mārgaguṇa kṣematamabhyupeti |emeva bhūmi prathamā
sthita bodhisattvaḥkṛtaniṣkramo daśabhibodhimupetyasaṅgaḥ

atra sthitā guṇadharā nṛpatī bhavantidharmānuśāsaka ahiṃsaka maitrayuktāḥ |jambudhvajaṃ sakalarājya
praśāsayantaḥsthāpenti tyāgi janatāṃ varabuddhajñāne

ākāṅkṣamāṇa vṛṣabhā vijahitva rājyaṃjinaśāsane upagatāścari ārabhantaḥ |labdhvā samādhiśata
buddhaśataṃ ca paśyikampenti kṣetraśatu bhāsi atikramanti

śodhyanti sattvaśata dharmamukhān viśantipraviśanti kalpaśatakāyaśataṃ nidarśi |pūrṇaṃ śataṃ
jinasutāna nidarśayantibhūyottari praṇidhiśreṣṭhabalāpramāṇāḥ

ityeṣā prathamā bhūmirnidiṣṭā sugatātmajāḥ |sarvalokahitaiṣīṇāṃ bodhisattvānanutamā
